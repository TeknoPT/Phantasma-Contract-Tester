How to use the Contract Tester.
How to Interact with the Contract Tester

Things to take in consideration before doing anything, you must choose if you are using your API, Localnet (Spook Running on your machine) or interacting with the Testnet or Mainnet.

  1. Select the desired API.
  2. Select the Contract, you want to test.
  3. Connect to the contract by Sign In with Poltergeist or Ecto.
  4. After Sign In to your Wallet the Status is still Not Connected, an error must occured, reload the page and try again.
  5. Select the desired method to test, and fill the parameters.
  6. Make the Call InvokeScript or a SendTransaction.
Tips

InvokeScript or InvokeRawScript are used to call the chain without changing it. (Only to get values from).

SendTransaction is used to call the chain when it changes something.
